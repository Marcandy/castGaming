    World of Tanks is a massively multiplayer online game developed by Belarusian-Cypriot[5] company Wargaming, featuring early to mid-20th century era fighting vehicles.[6] It is built upon a freemium business model where the game is free-to-play, but participants also have the option of paying a fee for use of "premium" features.
